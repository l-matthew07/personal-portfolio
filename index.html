About This Experience

Interactive Fluidity

This is a real-time, audio-reactive Navier-Stokes fluid simulation built by Pavel Dobryakov (PavelDoGreat), optimized for ultra-smooth performance and visual fidelity.

Controls:

  • Mouse: Create turbulent colorful trails. The speed of your mouse correlates to the force injected.
  • Audio Sync: Enabling this captures your system audio. Bass drives massive fluid eruptions, while highs modulate the luminescence and bloom intensity.

Features include Bloom, Sunrays, and realistic Shading for a premium "inky" appearance.

Computational Fluid Dynamics

A professional GPU solver performing millions of discrete calculations per frame to simulate incompressible fluid flow.

High-End Rendering

Incorporates multi-stage bloom, light scattering (Sunrays), and complex color blending architectures.

macOS Design System

A pixel-perfect recreation of the macOS dock and window management system, utilizing modern CSS glassmorphism and spring-based animations.
